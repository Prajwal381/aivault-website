Important: How to Use AI Ethically as a Student

⚠️ Don't submit AI-generated work as your own

Every tool in this list is meant to help you learn faster — not replace your learning. Use AI to understand concepts, brainstorm ideas, get feedback on your drafts, and practice. Submitting AI-written essays as your own is academic dishonesty at most institutions and risks serious consequences. Use these tools to become a better student, not to avoid being one.

The Best Free Student AI Stack (Zero Cost)

  • ChatGPT or Claude — explain concepts, help with outlines, answer questions
  • Perplexity AI — research with real citations
  • Grammarly — polish every piece of writing before submitting
  • Notion — organize all your notes and assignments (free for students)
  • Otter.ai — transcribe every lecture automatically
  • ElevenLabs — convert notes to audio for commute studying
  • GitHub Copilot — free AI coding assistant (CS students)

🎓 The honest student verdict

The students getting the most value from AI are using it as a tutor and thinking partner — not as a shortcut. Ask Claude to explain a concept five different ways until you understand it. Use Perplexity to explore research rabbit holes. Use Otter to never miss a lecture detail. Use ElevenLabs to turn your notes into a podcast you listen to while working out. These habits compound into real academic improvement.
